# Mahjong Tutorial

## Mahjong Tiles Representation

In Mahjong, each tile is represented by characters or numbers. Below is the notation used for the tiles:

### Numbered Tiles
- **1m** (1 of bamboo)
- **2m** (2 of bamboo)
- **3m** (3 of bamboo)
- **5m** (5 of bamboo)
- **1p** (1 of dots)
- **1p** (1 of dots)
- **3p** (3 of dots)
- **4p** (4 of dots)
- **5p** (5 of dots)
- **6p** (6 of dots)
- **7p** (7 of dots)

### Honor Tiles
- **西** (West Wind)
- **西** (West Wind)
